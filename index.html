About me.

My research interests are in probability and math biology, focusing on random trees and stochastic processes with applications to population genetics. I am currently PostDoc at TU Wien in Vienna, working at the Institue of Discrete Mathematics and Geometry with Benedikt Stufler.

I completed my PhD at the University of Uppsala under the supervision of Cecilia Holmgren and Svante Janson. Previously I was a PostDoc at the University of Vienna under the supervision of Emmanuel Schertzer.
