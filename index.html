About me

I am currently working as a PostDoc at the Institue of Discrete Mathematics and Geometry at TU Wien with Benedikt Stufler, working on limits of random networks.

Previously I was a PostDoc in the Biomathematics at the University of Vienna with Emmanuel Schertzer.

I completed my PhD under the supervision of Cecilia Holmgren and Svante Janson at Uppsala University, my doctoral thesis is titled Decorating trees grown in urns.

My masters studies were completed at the University of Manitoba under the supervision of David. S. Guderson, my masters thesis is titled On partition regular systems.

My research is mostly in probability, with a particular focus on population genetics and random trees.
